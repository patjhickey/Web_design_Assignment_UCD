ALL-IRELAND ROLL OF HONOUR


Kilkenny lead the way

Kilkenny leads the way with 36 titles, 10 of which were won in the last 14 years under the stewardship of Brian Cody, the most successful inter county manager in the GAA. Limerick are fourth in the league table with concentrated success in the 1930's and in the 2018 - 2022 timeframe.



Most Recent Limerick Successes. Why?

Four of the 11 successful triumphs came in the last 5 years. Many people have asked how come? The answer is simple. In the late 90s and early noughties Limerick poured a lot of effort into youth development. Traning academies and your development projects were put in place to capture and nurture talent. This began to pay off around 2015 and 2016 with many of the underage teams performing really well on the National stage. The big payoff happened n 2018 when the first All-Ireland title in 45 years was secured.
